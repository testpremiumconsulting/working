                          Financial Advisory Service

Premium Consulting provides financial advisory services in the area of Financial Management, Project Finance and Trade Finance. Our services include: 

  • Sourcing and negotiation of short to long term funds with financial institutions locally and abroad on the required financial capital. We work closely with local and offshore financial institutions that are looking for investment opportunity in the area of project finance, structured finance and trade finance with investment capacity of US$350,000 and above per project 
  • Reviewing of financial documentation of projects to ensure your interest is well protected. 
  •  Processing and acquisition of Banking License
  •  Monitoring disbursement and performance to ensure compliance with the terms and conditions of the facility.
