Financial Advisory Service

 Premium Consulting provides financial advisory services in the area of Financial Management, Project Finance and Trade Finance. Our services include:

Putting together appropriate financial structures based on evaluation of new and on-going projects.
Reviewing existing financial structure and recommending restructuring and realignment as appropriate.
Sourcing and negotiation of short to long term funds with financial institutions locally and abroad on the required financial capital. We work closely with local and offshore financial institutions that are looking for investment opportunity in the area of project finance, structured finance and trade finance with investment capacity of US$350,000 and above per project
Reviewing of financial documentation of projects to ensure your interest is well protected.
Monitoring disbursement and performance to ensure compliance with the terms and conditions of the facility.

Right Sizing and Optimization

We believe that every organisation should formulate plans to manage growth and at the same time cope with the shocks of variations in business cycles. In this regard we will advise you on the following:

.    Balance sheet restructuring, asset revaluation, right sizing and Portfolio optimization.
.    Cost reduction strategies to ensure that you are financed at the least possible total cost over the longest                        significant period.
.    Reviewing the financial needs of each company within your group and offering advice on the optimal financial     requirement over short, intermediate and long term period.
.    Suitability of the capital in your business (there should be harmony between the types of funds obtained and the     operating assets employed).
.    Manoeuvrability. An ability to be selective as to sources of future capital requirements [debt or equity] will     depend on maintaining debt levels below acceptable peaks.
.    Timing. In view of the fact that stocks and money market change constantly, we advise you on the timing of any     financial capital raising programme.
.    Income. A major objective is to provide a reasonable current yield as well as potential appreciation to the     shareholders. We offer advice on how to increase profit after tax by minimising direct and indirect costs of money     and credit.

Accounting Services

Our sister company, Olumuyiwa Iwayemi & Co. (Chartered Accountants) will provide you with Accounting Services which will be tailored to your specific needs. The services to be offered to you will be the following:

Design of Accounting and Allied Systems

We design and set up functional Accounting System tailored to suit your needs. In the case of an existing system, we can advise on changes to operational system that will enhance efficient service delivery. The essence of this service is to ensure that proper accounting records are being kept throughout the year. This will enable you to monitor closely your financial assets and liabilities and you will therefore be able to make better decisions on your companyâ€™s operations.

Having a functional accounting system will ensure key business performance analyses and reports are generated and circulated to the CEO and other business managers on a timely basis. It will also enable the implementation of budgetary mechanisms to proactively identify and manage budget/performance variances by the business units.

Premium Consulting will also offer advice on procurement of suitable accounting packages and computer hardware for your organisation. This service will be backed up by hands-on technical support to ensure smooth and stress-free implementation of the newly installed accounting system.


Internal Audit Services

To ensure the adequacy, integrity and reliability of your accounting records, there is the need to have a properly structured internal control system and regularly undertake an internal audit of your system. We will take the burden off your shoulders by providing you with comprehensive internal audit services. This will help you to ensure efficient and effective accounting system and reduce your cost of maintaining internal auditors within your system. We will therefore encourage you to outsource your internal audit service to save cost and optimise your system.

Reconciliation of Bank Statements and Accounts

Without a proper reconciliation of Bank Statements and Accounts, you could be losing money without knowing. When you engage our services, we shall carry out periodical reconciliation of your statements of accounts and advise you of discrepancies [if any] and remedial steps to be taken.

Compliance Reports

We offer audit and assurance services to enable you comply with regulatory requirements and give assurance to your stake holders and bankers about the financial status of your company. In addition, we prepare Accountants Reports for special purposes such as mergers and acquisition of other businesses, technical partnership, due diligence, new issues, private placements, re-organisation and right sizing and investigations into irregularities and fraud.
