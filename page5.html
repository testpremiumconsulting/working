Real Estate Services

We offer Real Estate Consulting & Management Services. Our sister company, Premium Homes Limited handles short & long term leases, letting, and sale of properties. We do business with Oil & Gas companies, Embassies, financial Institutions, commercial organisations and High Net-worth Individuals.

Premium Homes limited offers the following services:

A one stop shop for Nigerians resident abroad and in the country to have in one place all that they need to know about acquiring properties in the country, make property investment and possibly acquire additional cash flow that may arise from such investments.
Assistance to foreign individuals, companies and corporations that are operating or planning to establish business in Nigeria to acquire properties for their official use or for their staff.
Land acquisition and sale of properties.
Mortgage Brokerage and Insurance Services.
Buy-to-Let Leasing Services.

We would like you to contact us whenever you plan to make property acquisition or dispose of an existing property either for yourself or for your employees / directors.
