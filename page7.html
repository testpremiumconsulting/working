Founder / Chief Executive Officer – Muyiwa Iwayemi

Mr Iwayemi has at different times, been Secretary and President of the Nigerian Chapter of AIESEC Alumni Association. He has also served as Honorary Auditor of the Loyola Jesuit College, Abuja (the Lagos PTA). He is a member of the Nigerian Conservation Foundation and Musical Society of Nigeria. Mr Iwayemi is married with children.

He is the founder and Chief Executive of Premium Consulting, a business and financial advisory firm. Premium Consulting serves as a Business Service Provider/Consultant to the Overseas Private Investment Corporation (OPIC), the U.S. Government’s development finance institution which mobilizes private capital to help solve critical development challenges in emerging markets. Prior to the establishment of Premium Consulting, Mr. Iwayemi has had a successful career in Banking, Finance, and Accountancy while working at NAL Merchant Bank Plc, Industrial Bank Limited (Merchant Bankers), Banque Internationale du Benin and UAC of Nigeria Plc (“UACN”). He is currently the Managing Partner of Olumuyiwa Iwayemi & Co. (Chartered Accountants).

Mr. Iwayemi attended the University of Ibadan as a UACN Scholar and holds a B.Sc. [2nd Class Upper] degree in Economics (1979). He qualified as a Chartered Accountant under the UACN Accountants’ Training Scheme. He is a Fellow of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria and an Associate of the Chartered Institute of Taxation. He is an alumnus of the Kellogg Graduate School of Management, Northwestern University, Evanston, USA.

Ropo Ajayi
Business Partner

A Fellow of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria, and Associate Member of the Institute of Taxation of Nigeria, he graduated from the University of Ibadan in 1978. He is a member of the Business Recovery and Insolvency Practitioners Association of Nigeria. He is also a member of the Institute of Chartered Secretaries and Administrators of Nigeria.

He trained and practiced in the firm of Z.O.Ososanya & Co. (Chartered Accountants) from 1979 to 1987 where he rose to senior management level. He worked as an Accountant with Afro Continental Nigeria Limited from 1988 to 1990 after which he set up BOA Associates Limited [Management & Tax Consultants]. He has wide ranging experience covering financial & corporate management, Loan sourcing, auditing, taxation, debt recovery and receiver / manager.
